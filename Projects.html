Projects that I have recently worked on

Operation School Bells

Last spring, alongside a team of three other students, I served on a project to create a database for Assistance League of Norman, a non profit organization that serves families and children through community events. They were implementing an initiative called Operation School Bells that provides clothing and school supplies to children in a retail like environment. Based on their asks, my team and I utilized Microsoft SQL Server to create a database to better organize and store information about all the items that they would be providing to the children. We also created a series SQL statements to extract data from the database that was specific to the Client's needs.

The Hershey Company Pro Forma Model

In the fall of 2020, I was enrolled in a financial modeling course. Alongside two other team members, I created a pro forma financial model in Microsoft Excel that projected the future financial status of the company. I analyzed Hershey's current financial statements (balance sheet, income statement, and statement of cash flows) and used pertinent values such as projected cash flows, net profits, and taxes to summarize Hershey's future financial performance.
